Size guide
for B² apparel.

Our apparel is printed through Printify. Because each blank garment has its own cut and measurements, the safest fit check is always the size chart on the exact product you are ordering.

Shop the collection How to measure
Printify sizing

What this means

Printify provides a size guide for each product in its catalog. The chart explains how that specific garment is measured and how it is likely to fit.

01

Each product is different

A medium in one shirt style may not match a medium in another brand or blank.

02

Use garment measurements

Most charts measure the item itself, not your body. Compare it to a shirt you already like.

03

Expect small variation

Minor production differences can happen and are normal when they fall within Printify’s tolerance range.

How to measure

Compare before you order

MeasurementWhat it usually meansBest way to compare
WidthThe garment laid flat and measured across the chest area.Lay a shirt you already like flat and measure from armpit to armpit.
LengthThe garment measured from the upper shoulder or collar area down to the bottom hem.Measure a similar shirt from the top down to the bottom edge.
Sleeve lengthThe sleeve measurement shown for that specific garment style.Compare short sleeve to short sleeve, long sleeve to long sleeve, hoodie to hoodie, etc.
Fit notesSome product descriptions may mention whether an item runs smaller, looser, fitted, or relaxed.Use fit notes together with the measurement table before choosing your size.
Best tip: Do not choose only by your usual size label. Measure a garment you already own and like, then choose the product size closest to those measurements.
Sizing tolerance

Small differences are normal

Most garments

Up to 1 inch

Most Printify garments may vary by up to 1 inch, or 2.54 cm, from the listed size guide measurement.

Select popular blanks

Up to 1.5 inches

Bella+Canvas 3001, Gildan 5000, Comfort Colors 1717, Gildan 64000, Gildan 18000, and Gildan 18500 may vary by up to 1.5 inches, or 3.81 cm.

Baby garments

Up to 0.5 inches

Baby garments may vary by up to 0.5 inches, or 1.27 cm, from the listed size guide measurement.

If something is outside the expected tolerance: Contact us with your order information and clear photos of the garment laid flat with a measuring tape visible.
Before ordering

Final fit checklist

01

Open the exact product

Use the size chart for the specific item you want to buy.

02

Measure a similar item

Lay it flat and compare width, length, and sleeves where listed.

03

Choose your fit

When between sizes, decide whether you prefer a closer or roomier fit.

Source

This page summarizes Printify’s public size-guide guidance for shoppers. Printify says size guides are available for each product in the Printify Catalog, and sellers can add the size table to product descriptions when creating or editing products.
