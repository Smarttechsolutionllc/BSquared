Why we do it
A shirt can do more than look good.

Our Story: Bree and Brittany met online through a mutual friend and became friends quickly, never wavering in their support for one another. They later started their podcast, Two B's In A Podcast (available on Spotify), and shortly afterward watched a course on starting an LLC. Their reaction was simple: “How fun could this be?!” So they did it. Welcome to B Squared Apparel Plus, where two friends had a dream and brought it to life. We are happy you are here!
